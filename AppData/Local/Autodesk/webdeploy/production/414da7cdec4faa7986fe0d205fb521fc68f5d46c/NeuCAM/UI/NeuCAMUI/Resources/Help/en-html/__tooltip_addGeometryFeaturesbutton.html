Lets you select geometry from the canvas based on selection filters for quicker selections. The available selection filters may vary for each strategy.

In the example below, a Face Contours selections filter is used to click a face. The strategy automatically selects all the contours that are bound by the face.

Face Contours selection
  • Chain: Lets you select open or closed contours.
  • Face Contours: Lets you select a face, from which all the contours that are bound by the face are automatically selected.
  • Pockets: Lets you select open or closed pockets.
  • Silhouettes: Lets you select a body and use its silhouette, which is the boundary defined by the outline of a face or body, when viewed from the Z axis of the Work Cooridnate System (WCS).
  • Sketch Profiles: Lets you select sketches or parts of a sketch.
  • Pocket Recognition: Lets you automatically select pockets based on dimensions such as corner radii and pocket depth.

Tip: You can reuse geometry selections from other operations. To reuse the selections, with the Select button active, in the Browser, expand an operation's node then click the geometry selection.
